Go Fish!

Rules

  • Each player starts with 5 cards.
  • On your turn, ask another player for a rank you already have.
  • If they have any, they must give all cards of that rank — and you go again.
  • If not, "Go Fish!" — draw one card, and your turn ends.
  • Make sets of 4 matching ranks to score points.
  • If your hand is empty, you draw up to 4 cards from the deck on your turn.
  • Bot players remember past moves based on difficulty — choose wisely.
  • The game ends when all 13 sets are made or the deck is empty and no hands remain.
  • The player with the most sets wins.
  • Standard ranks (2–10, J, Q, K, A) are used; remember, suits don’t matter.
