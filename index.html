Go Fish!

Rules

  • Each player starts with 5 cards.
  • Ask another player for a card rank you already have.
  • If they have it, you get all cards of that rank.
  • If not — “Go Fish!” — draw from the deck.
  • Make sets (4 of a kind) to score points.
  • When all sets are made, the game ends.
  • Player with most sets wins!
